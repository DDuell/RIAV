Instructions

In this game, in each round you will be paired randomly and anonymously with another participant. One of you will be randomly designated Player 1 and the other will be designated Player 2. Prior to making a decision, you will learn your role.

In each round, in each pair, Player 1 can choose from two options: Left or Right. And, Player 2 can choose from two options: Up or Down.

Each of you must independently and simultaneously make a decision. Your payoffs will be determined by both choices and are given in a table such as shown below:

In each cell, the amount (in points) to the left is the payoff for Player 1 and to the right for Player 2. This table below is just an example of a potential realization of payoffs in a given round. In each round of the game, the exact payoffs will be different.

On your screen, you will be shown the payoffs for each round before and while you are making your choice.
